## Overview

Genesis empowers you to create stunning visual lessons and stories tailored to any audience. Whether you're crafting children's tales, sci-fi adventures, or brand narratives, the platform uses AI to generate professional ebooks with custom visuals. Follow these steps to get started quickly and launch your first project.

<Callout kind="tip">
  New users receive 5 free story generations to explore features without commitment.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Begin by signing up for a free account. Genesis supports multiple login methods for seamless access.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Signup" icon="mail">
    Visit `genesis.ai` and click **Sign Up**.

    1. Enter your email and create a strong password.
    2. Verify your email via the confirmation link.
    3. Complete the quick onboarding quiz about your preferred genres.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google" icon="google">
    Click **Continue with Google** on the login page.
    
    Genesis securely integrates with Google for instant access—no passwords needed.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Apple" icon="apple">
    Select **Sign in with Apple**.
    
    Your data remains private with Apple's privacy-focused authentication.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configure Initial Preferences

Set your default preferences to streamline future projects. These apply across all generations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings" icon="settings">
    Click your profile icon in the top-right and select **Preferences**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Genres" icon="genres">
    Select primary genres like `children`, `sci-fi`, or `educational`.

    ```json
    {
      "defaultGenre": "children",
      "targetAudience": "ages 4-8",
      "visualStyle": "watercolor"
    }
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Export Format" icon="download">
    Pick `PDF`, `EPUB`, or `interactive web` as your default output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Expandable title="Advanced Preferences" default-open="false">
  Customize AI parameters for more control:

  <CodeGroup tabs="JSON,YAML">
  ```json
  {
    "maxPages": 20,
    "imageResolution": "high",
    "narratorVoice": "friendly"
  }
  ```
  ```yaml
  maxPages: 20
  imageResolution: high
  narratorVoice: friendly
  ```
  </CodeGroup>
</Expandable>

## Launch Your First Project

Ready to create? Start a new visual story in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    Click **Create Story** on the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Prompt" icon="edit">
    Type a description: `"A curious fox discovers a hidden forest library and learns magic."`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate & Review" icon="zap">
    Select style, hit **Generate**, then edit text or visuals before exporting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Your first generation completes in `<60` seconds. Share via link or download as ebook.
</Callout>

Congratulations—you're now creating magical visual lessons with Genesis! Explore more in the dashboard to unlock advanced features like collaborative editing and custom AI training.
